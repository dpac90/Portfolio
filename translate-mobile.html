Translate.com

Native iOS / Android Apps

Period: October 2016 - September 2017
Client: Emerge Media
Role: Lead UI / UX Designer and Researcher
Project Type: Native iOS / Android Apps

Introduction

  • Translate.com for mobile is a language translation app that is available on iOS and Android
  • Supports input using text, handwriting, speech and photo and translation in over 90 language pairs

Problem Summary

  • Monthly active users (MAUs) were steadily declining
  • Engagement on the app was not as high as expected
  • Ratings on the app stores averaged around 3 stars

Tools and Technologies used:
Sketch, Axure, Zeplin, Adobe Photoshop, JIRA, Confluence, Slack, Skype

Results

  • New design led to year-over-year (YoY) increase of monthly active users by 116.62% on iOS and 350.68% on Android
  • Engagement on the app increased by 21.36%
  • Average app rating increased to 4.5 stars

Retrospective

  • The paper prototypes were very insightful. User testing on paper prototypes informed a lot of the early design
